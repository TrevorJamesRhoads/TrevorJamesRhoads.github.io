About Me

Trevor James Rhoads is a creative writer and music producer. He has spent far too much time designing this website to show off his stories and compositions but hopes you'll indulge him by taking a gander at his wares. Full transparency, this has been built in hopes to land creative gigs. Trevor is going through a bit of a third-life crisis and sees this as a reasonable avenue for success.

Trevor has spent the entirety of his life in the Ozarks, currently residing in Cotter, Arkansas, a quaint town nestled in a bend in the White River. He enjoys playing various instruments and being a feral caffeine goblin. I—umm, he—also has a particularly handsome dog named Hades.
