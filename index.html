About Me

Trevor James Rhoads is a creative writer and music producer. He has spent far too much time designing this website to show off his stories, but he hopes you'll indulge him by taking a gander at his wares. Full transparency, this has been built in hopes to land writing gigs. Trevor is going through a bit of a third-life crisis and sees this as a reasonable avenue for success.

Trevor has spent the entirety of his life in the Ozarks, currently residing in Cotter, Arkansas, a quiant town nestled in a bend of the White River. I—umm, he—has a particularly handsome dog named Hades.
